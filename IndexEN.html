DriveEduca

DriveEduca is dedicated to promoting good practices in road education through its website. It includes forums, chat, an interactive menu, contact platforms, and a search engine to access specific information. It offers an educational test and an interactive game to raise awareness about the risks of poor road education, thus promoting a safer road culture.

Role played: Front End Programmer

View Repository

PathToy

PathToy is a platform that emerged from a university project. It is a Web Application designed to facilitate the donation, exchange and request of toys between users from various communities. The main idea behind PathToy is to foster solidarity and promote the reuse of toys in good condition, thus contributing to the reduction of waste and the well-being of children of all ages.

Role played: Designer
