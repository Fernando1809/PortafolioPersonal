DriveEduca

DriveEduca is dedicated to promoting good practices in road education through its website. It includes forums, chat, an interactive menu, contact platforms, and a search engine to access specific information. It offers an educational test and an interactive game to raise awareness about the risks of poor road education, thus promoting a safer road culture.
